About Sharda University

Sharda University, located in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, is a leading educational institution known for its global outlook, interdisciplinary programs, and high academic standards. Accredited by NAAC and recognized by UGC, Sharda has established itself as a center for excellence in higher education and research.

The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in fields like engineering, business, law, medical sciences, design, and humanities. Sharda’s unique academic model is based on student-centric learning and international collaboration, attracting students from over 95 countries.

  • NAAC Accredited and UGC recognized
  • 700+ distinguished faculty and 250+ global partnerships
  • AICTE and NMC approved professional courses
  • Modern campus with AI labs, innovation hub, and smart classrooms
  • Online MBA and hybrid learning models available

Sharda’s emphasis on innovation, leadership, and global competence makes it a preferred destination for students aspiring to become future-ready professionals.
