About Delhi Public School, Birgunj

Delhi Public School, Birgunj is one of the premier educational institutions in Nepal affiliated with the globally recognized Delhi Public School Society of India. DPS Birgunj stands as a symbol of academic excellence, holistic development, and international standard learning within the Terai region of Nepal.

The school offers quality education from pre-primary to senior secondary levels, nurturing students through a curriculum designed to develop intellectual, physical, emotional, and social capabilities. With state-of-the-art facilities and a student-centric approach, DPS Birgunj ensures that students get the best blend of academics and co-curricular exposure.

The school follows the CBSE curriculum and prepares students for national and international benchmarks. It maintains high teaching standards with experienced faculty, smart classrooms, science and computer labs, a well-stocked library, and a focus on moral and ethical education.

Notable Features:

  • Affiliated with DPS Society, India
  • CBSE curriculum with NEB-recognized programs
  • Focus on creativity, leadership, and global citizenship
  • Modern campus infrastructure and disciplined environment
  • Participation in inter-DPS and national Olympiads

DPS Birgunj is dedicated to creating responsible future leaders with strong academic foundations and ethical values.
