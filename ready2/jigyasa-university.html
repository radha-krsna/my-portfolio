About Jigyasa University

Jigyasa University, located in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, is a modern private university recognized for its innovation in education, dynamic academic environment, and commitment to nurturing leadership in every student.

The university offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses including BBA, MBA, BCA, and various science and arts programs. Jigyasa stands out for integrating practical experience with academic learning, promoting research, and encouraging entrepreneurship among students.

  • Focus on experiential and project-based learning
  • Modern campus with research centers and tech-enabled classrooms
  • Internship-driven curriculum with strong industry tie-ups
  • Vibrant student community and active placement cell
  • NAAC-accredited programs with UGC recognition

Jigyasa University’s motto is to turn curiosity into capability, fostering innovation and confidence in students from across India.
